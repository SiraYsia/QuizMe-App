How to get started:

For more accurate flashcards that cover at least 99% of the material, please input shorter and more concise texts. You will have the option to append to your existing flashcard if you need to add more texts later.

The shorter your text the more flashcards you can get out of it.

Please note: The flashcard generation process may take a few tries to format output correctly. If you encounter empty flashcards after clicking on "Create Flashcards" and specifying the number of flashcards, simply try the process again. We are working on making it even more seamless.
